Venice (Venezia in Italian) is one of those places that — if you're going to Italy — you simply cannot and should not miss. Possibly the most romantic city in the world, Venice is (as far as I know) utterly unique: they don't make cities out of winding canals and thin-as-can-be cobbled alleyways and bridges anymore; and let's face it, they never really did earlier in history, either.

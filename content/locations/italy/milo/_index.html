No, I don't mean the chocolate-flavoured energy drink: I mean Milo, the small mountain village on the eastern edge of Sicily, between towering Mount Etna (Europe's highest active volcano) and the gorgeous Ionian coast. I slept at a campsite just outside of Milo this evening, and I rode through the village the following morning.

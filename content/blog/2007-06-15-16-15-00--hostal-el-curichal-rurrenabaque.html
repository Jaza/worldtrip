Very comfortable guesthouse, just outside of the busy part of Rurrenabaque, with a very nice atmosphere, and a great balcony full of lazy hammocks to crash in. Stayed here in between my jungle and pampas trips in the area, and it was nice and cosy every time.

Rooms: clean, respectable, and quiet.

People: generally too tired and chilled to chat much, but a very nice crowd all-round.

Staff: a bit brisk and business-like, but accommodating enough.

Location: a few streets away from the main part of Rurrenabaque: much quieter than other places, and still close enough to everything.

Food / Internet: none.

Hot water: reliable and luxurious.

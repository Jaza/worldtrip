This is a great little place to stay in Xalapa, although it was very quiet and empty, which is apparently how it is most of the time.

Rooms: nice and clean.

People: not many, but those that are there are great — some are a bit rugged.

Staff: nice, but non-English-speaking.

Location: middle of town, a short walk from everything.

Food: simple, free breakfast.

Internet: free for 15 mins (only 1 PC).
